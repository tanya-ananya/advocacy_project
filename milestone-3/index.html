About My Topic

Working as a book influencer for a major publishing company, I've noticed that many Asian authors don’t receive the right type of support or marketing for their books. This website will focus solely on Asian authors’ books–to deliver a range of interactions from fan content to my professional works as an influencer.

How Can This Initiative Benefit Authors and Readers?

  • Increase outreach to target audiences via social media
  • Connect readers with books that represent them
  • Highlight Asian authors and their books

Where Can Authors Benefit From This Initiative?

Social Media

By encouraging influencers or "bookstagrammers" and "booktokers", the use of social media to drive marketing for an author's intended audience can increase outreach.
